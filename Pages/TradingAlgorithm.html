AI Stock Trading Project (Work in Progress)

An AI-driven trading algorithm designed to analyze market data and execute trades.









































About The Project

This project is an AI-based stock trading algorithm developed as a personal endeavor to explore algorithmic trading. The system is built upon the Pybroker framework and is written in Python. The*integrates various frameworks and APIs to analyze market data, make predictions, and execute trades automatically*. Using advanced machine learning techniques, the algorithm continuously learns from market trends and adjusts its strategies to maximize returns while minimizing risks.

Features

Market Analysis

Utilizes advanced algorithms to analyze market trends and make informed trading decisions.

Automated Trading

Executes trades automatically based on predefined strategies and market conditions.

Customizable Strategies

Allows users to customize trading strategies based on their risk tolerance and investment goals.

Data Integration

Integrates with various APIs to fetch real-time market data and historical prices.

In Reflection

What I Learned

This project provided valuable insights into the world of algorithmic trading and the complexities involved in developing a trading system. I gained hands-on experience with data analysis, machine learning, and API integration.

Challenges Faced

One of the main challenges was ensuring the algorithm could adapt to changing market conditions. I had to refine my strategies continuously and backtest them rigorously to achieve consistent results.

Future Improvements

In the future, I plan to enhance the algorithm's predictive capabilities by incorporating more advanced machine learning techniques and expanding the range of assets it can trade.
